Biography

I am now a 3rd-year M.S student at Shenyang Aerospace University, supervised by Prof. Yufeng Li and worked closely with Dr. Yufeng Huang. I will graduate in 2022.06 and then join the Intelligent Media Analysis Group, Nanjing University of Science and Technology.

My research interests include image dehazing/deraining, image enhancement/restoration, and related low-level vision problems. The ultimate goal of my research is to make the world look clearer. If you want to cooperate with me, kindly contact me via email.
