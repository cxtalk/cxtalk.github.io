Biography

I am a M.S student at the College of Electronic Information Engineering, Shenyang Aerospace University (SAU), co-advised by Prof. Yufeng Li and Dr. Yufeng Huang. I will graduate in 2022.06 and plan to join Intelligent Media Analysis Group (IMAG).

I am engaged in image processing and computer vision related research and work. My research interests include image restoration, enhancement, reconstruction, and other low-level vision problems.

My vision is to make the world look clearer. I am looking for several collaborators in low-level vision community. If you are interested, kindly contact me via email.
